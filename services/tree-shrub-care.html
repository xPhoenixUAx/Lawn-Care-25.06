Tree and shrub care

Sharper shrubs, cleaner beds, and healthier ornamental growth.

We prune, shape, and clean up small landscape plants so beds look intentional and remain easier to maintain.

Careful trimming that respects the plant and the property.

Good shrub care is not just cutting everything into a square. We consider plant type, bloom timing, size, sight lines, walkways, windows, air flow, and how clippings will be removed from beds and turf.

Included tasks

  • Hedge shaping and light ornamental pruning
  • Removal of dead, crossing, or awkward small branches where accessible
  • Clearance around walkways, windows, driveways, and entry areas
  • Clipping collection from turf, mulch, patios, and sidewalks
  • Plant condition notes for stress, crowding, or watering issues
  • Coordination with mulch refresh and seasonal cleanup

Best timing

Timing depends on plant type and goals. Some shrubs are best shaped after flowering, while others can be maintained during active growth. We schedule work to keep the property neat without removing the best seasonal display unnecessarily.

What is not included

Large tree removal, high climbing, utility line work, and hazardous limbs require a qualified tree service. We focus on accessible ornamental trees, shrubs, hedges, and landscape bed plants.
