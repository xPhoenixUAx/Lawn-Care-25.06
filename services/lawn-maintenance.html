Lawn maintenance

Consistent mowing and finish work that keeps the whole property sharp.

Our recurring maintenance service is built for homeowners who want clean turf, crisp edges, tidy hard surfaces, and useful visit notes without managing every detail themselves.

What this service covers

Lawn maintenance includes more than running a mower over the grass. We match mowing height to turf conditions, trim around fences and beds, edge visible hardscape lines, clear clippings from walks and drives, and watch for changes that may require repair or treatment.

The goal is a lawn that looks cared for after every visit while avoiding stress from cutting too short, repeated rutting, missed corners, or debris left behind.

Included tasks

  • Scheduled weekly or biweekly mowing based on growth rate
  • String trimming around trees, fences, posts, and bed borders
  • Mechanical edging along driveways, sidewalks, patios, and curb lines
  • Blowing clippings from hard surfaces after the cut
  • Basic weed and turf observations documented during service
  • Seasonal height adjustments during heat, rain, and slower growth periods

Best for

This service is ideal for busy homeowners, rental properties, recently renovated yards, established lawns that need steady presentation, and households that want one reliable crew watching the lawn throughout the season.

How we protect your property

Crews confirm gate access, identify irrigation heads where visible, avoid wet areas that could rut, and keep equipment movement organized. If conditions are too wet for a clean cut, we recommend adjusting the visit rather than damaging the lawn.
