Seasonal cleanups

Reset the yard before debris turns into turf damage.

Spring and fall cleanups remove the material that blocks light, traps moisture, hides edges, and makes regular maintenance harder.

Cleanup service with a lawn health purpose

Leaves, branches, old mulch, dead annuals, and winter debris can smother grass and create messy bed lines. Our seasonal cleanups clear the property so mowing, fertilization, and plant care can work properly again.

We can schedule cleanups as one-time resets or pair them with recurring maintenance before and after peak leaf drop.

Included cleanup tasks

  • Leaf collection from lawn areas, beds, patios, and visible edges
  • Small branch and storm debris pickup
  • Bed cleanup around shrubs, perennials, and foundation plantings
  • Cutback of selected spent plant material when appropriate
  • Hardscape blowing and final property sweep
  • Recommendations for mulch refresh, pruning, or turf repair after cleanup

Spring cleanup

Spring visits remove winter debris, open up beds, identify damaged turf, and prepare the lawn for mowing and fertilization. This is a good time to note bare areas, drainage marks, and shrub damage from snow or wind.

Fall cleanup

Fall service prevents heavy leaf cover from blocking sunlight and holding moisture on the grass. Depending on tree coverage, we may recommend multiple passes instead of waiting until the yard is buried.
