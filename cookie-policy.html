How this website may use cookies.

This website is intentionally lightweight and does not include analytics or advertising pixels unless they are specifically provided and approved.

What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored by a browser to help websites remember settings, support security, or understand basic site usage.

Current cookie use

This static website does not intentionally set marketing cookies, tracking pixels, Google Analytics IDs, Google Tag Manager IDs, Meta Pixel IDs, TikTok Pixel IDs, or ad conversion IDs.

Third-party resources

The site may load fonts from Google Fonts. Your browser may make requests to that provider when loading typefaces. If additional third-party tools are added later, this policy should be updated to describe them.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect some websites, although this website is designed to remain usable without optional cookies.
