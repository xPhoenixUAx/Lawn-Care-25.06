Before you hire

What homeowners should confirm with any provider.

GreenScape Lawn Care Connect helps with the connection step. The final hiring decision always belongs to the homeowner, so the conversation with a provider should be specific before work begins.

  • Confirm license and insurance requirements for the work
  • Ask what is included, excluded, and priced separately
  • Clarify timing, access, pets, gates, parking, and cleanup
  • Review photos, measurements, or site details before approval
  • Keep the provider's written scope or estimate for reference
Better requests

Details that help providers respond faster.

A clear request helps local providers understand the job before they call, which can reduce back-and-forth and make the first conversation more useful.

  • Photos of the whole yard and close-ups of problem areas
  • Approximate size, gate access, pets, parking, and timing needs
  • Whether the job is urgent, one-time, recurring, or seasonal
  • Any known irrigation, drainage, slope, or damaged turf concerns
