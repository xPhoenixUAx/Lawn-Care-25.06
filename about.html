About us

A faster way to find local lawn care providers.

GreenScape Lawn Care Connect is a free connection service for homeowners who need help finding independent local providers for lawn and yard work.

Our story

Built for homeowners tired of calling around.

GreenScape Lawn Care Connect started from a simple problem: when a yard needs attention, homeowners often have to search directories, leave voicemails, compare scattered listings, and guess which provider handles the exact job.

We do not provide lawn care services ourselves. Instead, we organize the request and help homeowners connect with independent local contractors and providers who may be able to respond to mowing, cleanup, landscaping, irrigation, turf health, shrub care, or sod repair needs.

  • Free request intake for homeowners
  • Connections based on service category and local area
  • Homeowners review, choose, and verify any provider they hire
What we do

We make the first step simpler and clearer.

Our role is to help move a homeowner from "I need help" to "I know who I can talk to next."

Collect

We gather the service type, property details, timing, access notes, and contact information needed to route the request.

Connect

We help match requests with independent local providers who handle the relevant lawn care category.

Clarify

Providers can discuss scope, pricing, availability, access, and job-specific requirements directly with the homeowner.

Verify

The homeowner is responsible for confirming license, insurance, qualifications, and final hiring decisions.

Connection support

What homeowners can use us for.

  • Finding provider options for common lawn and yard requests
  • Organizing project details before the first provider conversation
  • Reducing the time spent searching across scattered listings
  • Starting with one request for recurring, seasonal, or project work
Important distinction

What we do not claim to do.

We are not the contractor performing the work, and we do not warrant or guarantee any work performed by independent providers.

  • We do not set final prices, schedules, or service terms
  • We do not replace homeowner due diligence
  • We do not guarantee licensing, insurance, or work outcomes
How we think

The request should be easy, but the hiring choice should stay careful.

We built the site around clear expectations: fast intake, local provider options, and homeowner control over the final decision.

Specific beats vague

Photos, access notes, timing, and service categories help providers understand the request before they call.

Local context matters

Lawn problems can depend on season, soil, irrigation, property access, and neighborhood conditions.

Choice stays with you

Homeowners decide who to speak with, what estimate to accept, and which provider to hire.

Responsible matching

Better connections start with better information.

The more complete the request, the easier it is for a provider to understand whether the work fits their services, schedule, and area.

Before hiring anyone, homeowners should ask direct questions and confirm any legal or insurance requirements for the work being performed.

  • Share the service type and any urgency up front
  • Add photos of the whole yard and problem areas when possible
  • Ask what is included, excluded, and priced separately
  • Confirm license and insurance requirements before work begins
  • Keep written scope, estimate, and scheduling details for reference
