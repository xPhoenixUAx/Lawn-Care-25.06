Privacy Policy

How we handle homeowner request information.

This policy explains how collects and uses information for a free lawn care provider connection service.

Overview of our privacy practices

operates as a free connection service that helps homeowners submit lawn and yard requests and connect with independent local service providers. Because our role is to route requests and support communication, the information you provide may need to be shared with providers or communication partners who can respond to your request.

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through this website, forms, phone calls, emails, texts, and related communications connected to a service request. It does not control the privacy practices of independent providers after they receive your information or communicate with you directly.

Information you provide directly

We may collect information you choose to submit, including your name, phone number, email address, service address or general location, requested service category, message content, timing preferences, property access notes, photos, and details about lawn or yard conditions.

Examples of property details may include lawn size, grass height, debris volume, bare patches, irrigation concerns, gates, pets, parking, slopes, damaged turf, landscape beds, shrubs, cleanup needs, or other notes that help identify the right provider category.

Information collected automatically

Like many websites, basic technical information may be collected automatically when you visit the site. This can include browser type, device type, approximate location derived from network information, referring page, pages viewed, date and time of visit, and basic server log information. This information helps operate, secure, and improve the website.

This site is intentionally lightweight and does not currently describe itself as using advertising pixels or analytics tags unless they are later added and disclosed. See the Cookie Policy for more detail.

How we use information

We use information to receive and organize service requests, identify relevant service categories, contact you about your request, route request details to independent providers, support follow-up communication, improve website clarity, prevent misuse, maintain records, and comply with applicable obligations.

We may also use request details in an internal, aggregated, or de-identified way to understand common homeowner needs, improve service category descriptions, and make the request process clearer. Aggregated or de-identified information does not identify a specific homeowner.

Sharing with independent providers

To provide the connection service, we may share your request information with independent local contractors, companies, providers, or related communication partners who may be able to respond to your lawn or yard request. This sharing can include your contact information and the request details needed for a provider to evaluate whether to contact you.

Independent providers are not controlled by this website. If you speak with, hire, or pay a provider, that provider may collect additional information from you and may have its own privacy practices. You should ask providers directly about how they handle your information if that is important to you.

Other sharing

We may share information with vendors who help operate the website, host data, manage communications, provide form or phone support, secure systems, or maintain business records. We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, governmental request, fraud prevention, safety concerns, dispute handling, or to protect rights, property, users, providers, or the website.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense of exchanging it for money. Because privacy laws can define sale, sharing, or targeted advertising differently, users with specific legal rights may contact us using the email below.

Communications

When you submit a request, you authorize us and relevant independent providers or communication partners to contact you about that request by phone, text, email, or other reasonable communication method. Message and data rates may apply. You may ask us to stop contacting you, and you may also need to make opt-out requests directly to any provider that has already received your information.

Data retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to requests, maintain business records, improve the website, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, prevent misuse, and document communications. Retention periods may vary depending on request type, communication history, and operational needs.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect submitted information. No website, email, phone, or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure. Please do not submit highly sensitive information such as financial account numbers, government identification numbers, medical information, passwords, or security codes through general request forms.

Your choices and requests

You may contact us at to request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of information associated with your request, subject to reasonable verification, provider-routing needs, legal obligations, recordkeeping requirements, and legitimate business purposes.

If your information has already been shared with an independent provider, we may not be able to delete or control copies held by that provider. You should contact the provider directly for requests involving information they maintain.

Children's privacy

This website is intended for homeowners and adults seeking provider connections. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child submitted information, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Third-party websites and providers

This website may link to pages, services, or providers that we do not operate. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, security, or business practices of third parties. Review any provider's policies and terms before sharing additional information or hiring them.

Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions are effective when posted unless a later effective date is stated. Continued use of the website after an update means the current policy applies to information collected going forward.
